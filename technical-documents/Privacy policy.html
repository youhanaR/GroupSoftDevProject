Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [23/2/2025]

  1. Introduction
    Welcome to the Sustainability Gamification Web App, developed by the JARSE Group as part of the ECM2434 Group Software Engineering Project at the University of Exeter. This app is designed to promote sustainable behaviours on campus through gamification. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This document (privacy policy) outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.
  2. Information We Collect
    We may collect the following information:
  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and university affiliation.
  • Location Data: GPS location or QR codes littered around certain spots on campus to verify presence.
  • Profile Information: User type, progress, achievements, and leaderboard status.
  • User-Generated Content: Photos submitted as part of gameplay challenges.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and IP address.
  1. How We Use Your Information
    We use your information to:
  • Authenticate your account and provide access to features.
  • Track progress, verify locations, and manage leaderboards.
  • Enhance gameplay through personalized experiences.
  • Monitor app performance and improve usability.
  1. Sharing Your Information
    We do not sell your data. Information may be shared with:
  • University of Exeter staff for administrative and research purposes.
  • Authorized developers for app maintenance and updates.
  • GDPR-compliant third-party services for hosting and data storage.
  1. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
    We use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyse app usage, and improve functionality.
  2. Data Security
    We implement encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your data.
  3. Data Retention
    Data is retained only as long as necessary for the app’s functionality or as required by law. Accounts inactive for 12 months will be deleted.
  4. Your Privacy Rights
    You have the right to:
  • Access and obtain a copy of your data.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request the deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  1. Children’s Privacy
    The app is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
