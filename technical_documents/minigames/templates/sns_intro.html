Sort n Serve

In Sort n Serve, your mission is to quickly sort and serve falling items to their correct destinations. Items such as plates, utensils, waste and food will appear on a conveyor belt. Send each item to the appropriate target area before it reaches the bottom. Earn points for every correct sort and avoid mistakes!

How to Play

  • Each correctly sorted item earns you 10 points; an incorrect sort deducts 5 points.
  • Items fall from the conveyor beltâ€”act quickly before they reach the bottom.
  • If an item falls unsorted or the timer runs out of time, the game ends.
  • The game speeds up over time, so your reaction time is key.
  • Send items to the correct areas:

    • Customer Area:Foods in Bowls and Plates, Cups of Tea
    • Sink :Plates, Forks, Spoons
    • Food Waste Recycling Bin:Fish Bones, Cracked Eggs, Eaten Apples
    • Mixed Recycling Bin:Styrofoams, Crushed Cans, Pizza Boxes
