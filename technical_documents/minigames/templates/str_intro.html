Sort the Recycling

Welcome to Sort the Recycling!

  • Sort the Recycling is a match-three game where you sort groups of recycling into rows and columns (min 3).
  • 3 in a row is 1 point, more than 3 is 3 points, and getting a vertical and horizontal combo is 5 points.
  • You must also deal with rubbish. Rubbish cannot be sorted, and must instead be removed from the grid.
  • You gain the ability to remove the rubbish by completing tasks. Your current task will display at the bottom of the screen.
  • You must reach the target of 50 points before your 15 turns run out.

Good luck.
