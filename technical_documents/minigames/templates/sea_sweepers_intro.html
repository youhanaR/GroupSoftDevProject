Sea Sweepers

Welcome to Sea Sweepers!

  • Sea Sweepers is an underwater action game where you control a yellow rubber duck to clean up waste in water.
  • Collect trash (e.g., soda cans, water bottles, batteries) to earn 10 points per item.
  • Avoid marine life, as colliding with any creature ends the game.
  • As you progress, the waters become more crowded with both trash and marine life, increasing the difficulty.
  • Play continues until you either collide with marine creatures or the 60-second timer runs out.
  • Your final score reflects how much pollution you managed to clean up.

Good luck.
