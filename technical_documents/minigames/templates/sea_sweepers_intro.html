Sea Sweepers

In Sea Sweepers, you control a yellow rubber duck to clean up waste floating around underwater.

How to Play

  • Collect trash (soda cans, water bottles, batteries) for 10 points each.
  • Avoid marine life! Colliding with any creature ends the game.
  • The waters get more crowded with trash and marine life as you progress, raising the difficulty.
  • Play continues until you collide with a creature or the 60-second timer runs out.
  • Your final score reflects how much pollution you cleaned up.
  • To move the duck:

    • On desktop: Use either the on-screen arrow buttons or your keyboard arrow keys.
    • On mobile: Use the on-screen arrow buttons.
