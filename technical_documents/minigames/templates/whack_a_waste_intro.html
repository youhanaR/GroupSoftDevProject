Whack-A-Waste

In this game, you manage food items to minimise waste by making quick decisions on how to handle each one.

How to Play

  • Food items, represented by coloured circles, appear at various stages of their shelf life, and you must decide what action to take.
    • Fresh: Don't tap
    • Expiring: Tap
    • Rotten: Tap
    • Trash: Don't tap
  • As the game progresses, more food items appear, and the time to make decisions shortens, increasing the challenge.
  • Good decisions add to your points, wasteful choices deduct them.
  • The game ends when you make 5 mistakes.
