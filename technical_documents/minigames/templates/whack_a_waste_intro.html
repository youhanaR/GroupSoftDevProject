Whack-A-Waste

Welcome to Whack-A-Waste!

  • In this game, you manage food items to minimize waste by making quick decisions on how to handle each one.
  • Food items appear at various stages of their shelf life, and you must decide what action to take before they spoil.
  • As the game progresses, more food items appear, and the time to make decisions shortens, increasing the challenge.
  • Make good decisions to earn rewards; make wasteful choices and face penalties.
  • The game ends when you interact wrongly with a set number of food items.

Good luck.
