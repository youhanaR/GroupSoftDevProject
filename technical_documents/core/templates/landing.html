Team up with our favourite feline to clean up Streatham Campus through fun, fast-paced mini-games.

Why It Matters

Aligned with SDG #12: Responsible Consumption & Production and the University of Exeter’s Circular Economy and Sustainable Resource Management Strategy, this game empowers you to:

  • Transform daily routines into active learning:
    Integrate sustainability into everyday life through a fun, treasure-hunt-style approach.
  • Tailored educational experiences:
    Location-specific minigames offer real-world context that educates on recycling and its environmental impact.
  • Foster ownership and accountability:
    Gain a deeper understanding of your role in sustainability and how you can contribute to the university’s larger goals.
  • Empower community-driven environmental action:
    Encourages proactive steps toward addressing campus sustainability, fostering a lasting culture of environmental stewardship.
